About The Department

The department of Computer Science and Engineering is functioning since the inception of the college in 1998. B.E CSE Programme is accredited by NBA in the revised norms and the department has permanent affiliation to Anna University.It is also a recognized research centre by Anna University. The department offers three programmes namely, B.E (CSE), M.E(CSE) and Ph.D / M.S By Research. The department is ranked in top 65 positions by AICTE-CII Survey for instilling Industry-Institute Partnership in the year 2017 & 2018 the department is ranked among top 20% (Platinum Category) of the 9000+ institutions that applied for the AICTE CII Survey.

The department has been doing extremely well in the academic year 2019-2020. Being accredited by NBA for the fourth time, the department has grown its programs to extend across business information systems, networking, cyber security, data science, programming and software development, web design and development, as well as game design and development. For those with wider interests, we also focus on innovation and entrepreneurship. Around 78 % of the eligible students are placed in reputed MNC, 15% of the students are admitted in premier institutions in India and Abroad for Masters Programme and 2 teams have started their own businesses as registered ‘startup companies’.

Our department is reflective of our active and thriving broader community that covers interests across a diverse selection of computing areas. From language technology, cryptography, security, programming languages, advanced databases, big data analytics, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, knowledge systems and optimization techniques, our focus remains fixated on achieving teaching excellence to both undergraduate and postgraduate students. Supported by our many inter-departmental collaborations and industry engagements, the Department of CSE at KCG campus is committed to continue to provide endless opportunities for all involved.

About The Symposium

CSEISMIC 2020 is a national level technical symposium organized by the department of CSE in association with ISTE Student Chapter scheduled on the 10th January 2020. This symposium is a carnival for all those engineering students out there who are interested in learning new things and are looking forward to enlighten their knowledge. This fest will have various technical, series of lectures, seminars and exhibitions which will enrich an engineer with advanced knowledge in their field and attract students of various disciplines from all over India. CSEISMIC has been improving its standards to meet its tag ENVISION THE CHANGE.

About ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System. At present, ISTE has a very large and an effective membership base consisting of more than 1,02,985 Life Members, 5,54,094 Student Members, 2410 Institutional Members (including IITs, IISc.., NITs and other leading technical institutions), 1214 Faculty Chapters and 1322 Students’ Chapters and 17 Sections throughout the country.

The major objective of the ISTE is to provide quality training programmes to teachers and administrators of technical institutions to update their knowledge and skills in their fields of activity and to assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industry and other organisations.

TECHNOSTORM

Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation


Description

Research is to see what everybody else has seen and to think what nobody else has thought.Do you believe that your bleeding-edge concept could be the next breakthrough innovation? Then come present your paper at KCG College of Technology

Rules

    1. Maximum number of participants in a Team is 3.
    2. Paper should be in IEEE format and plagiarism is not allowed.
    3. Selected teams should bring your PowerPoint presentation in a pen-drive.
    4. The teams will get 8 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
    5. Please Mail your abstract and paper to (csesymposium@kcgcollege.com).
    6. Selected teams should bring 2 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
    7. Topics can be based on any domain related to CSE.
    8. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
    9. Last date for submission is 6th January 2020 and Last date for intimating the acceptance is 7th January 2020.

Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt


Description

A modern treasure hunt, sometimes called a scavenger hunt. Your team will be given one clue leads to another. Using the given clues you need to find the final treasure.

Rules

    1. A team must consist of maximum of two members.
    2. Usage of the internet for references is not allowed.
    3. Totally 5(4+1) clues will be given to each team.
    4. There may also be a number of spurious treasures planted in likely locations to discourage teams from hoarding treasures.
    5. The Treasure Hunt consists of clues, each one leading to the next clue.

    7. Never tamper with another team's treasure.When you pick up your own team's treasure at a location, leave the other treasures hidden there exactly as you found them.
    8. A map of the college will be provided to each team.
